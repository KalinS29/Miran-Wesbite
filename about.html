Our Story

Trusted Livestock Partners Since 2010

Miran Livestock is a EU-certified livestock trading and export company headquartered in Razgrad, Bulgaria. We specialize in connecting quality cattle, sheep, and goats with international markets.

Our operations span the entire livestock export chain—from sourcing through our extensive network of trusted European suppliers, to veterinary certification, quarantine management, and final delivery to destinations across the Middle East, North Africa, and beyond.

With over 15 years of experience, we have built a reputation for reliability, transparency, and excellence in animal welfare. Our team of veterinarians, logistics specialists, and trading experts work together to ensure every export meets the highest standards.

Our Mission

Excellence in Every Export

We believe that quality livestock export requires more than just logistics—it demands dedication to animal welfare, strict compliance with international regulations, and genuine partnerships with our clients.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between European livestock producers and international markets, ensuring that every animal we export arrives healthy, documented, and ready for its new environment.
