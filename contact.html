FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does the export process take?

+

The typical export process takes 2-4 weeks from order confirmation to delivery. This includes sourcing, veterinary checks, quarantine (7-14 days depending on destination), and transport arrangements.

What documents do you provide?

+

We provide all necessary export documentation including: Veterinary Health Certificate, EU Export Certificate, Origin Certificate, CITES permits (if required), and customs documentation for the destination country.

Which countries do you export to?

+

We regularly export to UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Jordan, Libya, and other Middle Eastern and North African countries. We can also arrange exports to other destinations based on your requirements.

What is the minimum order quantity?

+

We accommodate orders of various sizes. While we specialize in commercial volumes (50+ head), we can discuss smaller orders depending on the destination and current availability. Contact us for specific requirements.

How do you ensure animal welfare during transport?

+

Animal welfare is our top priority. We use EU-approved transport vehicles with proper ventilation, spacing, and rest stops. All journeys comply with EU animal transport regulations, and we provide veterinary supervision for longer routes.

What payment terms do you offer?

+

We typically require a 30% deposit upon order confirmation, with the remaining 70% due before shipment. For established partners, we can discuss alternative payment arrangements including letters of credit.
